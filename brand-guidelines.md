# Hashkies Brand Guidelines

## Brand Personality
Authentic, Modern, Fast, Reliable, and Warm. Hashkies brings traditional East African comfort food (Anjera & Mufo) into the modern Nairobi lifestyle.

## Logo
- **Main Logo**: `hashkies-logo.png`
- **Favicon**: `favicon.png`
- **Usage**: The logo should be used on light backgrounds. For dark backgrounds, use a version with white text (if applicable).

## Color Palette
- **Terracotta (Primary)**: `#D2691E` - Warmth, spice, and tradition.
- **Charcoal (Secondary)**: `#333333` - Modernity, professionalism, and contrast.
- **Cream (Background/Neutral)**: `#F5F5DC` - Natural bread tones, warmth, and accessibility.
- **Leaf Green (Accent)**: `#2E8B57` - Freshness and organic ingredients.

## Typography
- **Headings**: Modern Bold Sans-Serif (e.g., Montserrat, Poppins, or Roboto).
- **Body**: Clean Sans-Serif (e.g., Open Sans or Lato).

## Visual Style
- Clean, minimalist graphics.
- High-quality food photography with warm lighting.
- Focus on the textures of Anjera and Mufo.
